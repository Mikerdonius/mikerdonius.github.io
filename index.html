Welcome to the IT landscape dashboard. This dashboard should be used to get a clear view of ones company IT landscape. Please follow the steps below:

Step 1 – Place the blocks that are used within your company on their desired place by drag and dropping. When u are not certain, please highlight the block by clicking one time and research if the block is used or not. The blocks can be resized as of your liking. To get more information about the block itself, please double click on the block.

Step 2 – When a block is not used, put this block within the canvas. This canvas acts as a bin, but could also show if your company should investigate if this block is useful.

Step 3 – When blocks are missing u can add blocks yourself via the “Add block” button. White blocks act as systems, while the orange blocks are functions and processes.

Step 4 – Walk through your company’s IT landscape.

Ctrl + R Could be used to refresh the dashboard.
Ctrl + S Could be used to save the webpage for later use.
Ctrl + P Could be used to print the dashboard.

This dashboard has been made by four students of the “Hogeschool Rotterdam” in a project of the minor “Innovative logistics and IT” commissioned by TLN. The dashboard acts as a proof of concept that will be improved in the future.
