The Government Digipoort is an ICT centre that provides a digital way of reporting and documenting with the government. Certain processes within the logistical environment have to be indicated to the government. One of such processes is customs declaration. The government has provides a platform where companies have an easier method of sharing this data. This way processes become more automated, thus improve optimization in time, quality and reliability.

Portbase is a platform for logistical service providers to communicate with each other about shipments. Portbase is related to shipping- and transport companies. The data inserted by companies can be consulted by other companies. This creates a transparent overview, which in turn provides easier access of data. Portbase is founded by “Harbour company Rotterdam” as it has interest in improving the harbour.

A WarehouseManagement System controls and keeps track of al the information and goods flows within warehouses. The main three components of WMS consist of: Lay-out and material handling, inbound and order picking, and financial administrating. The goal of a WMS is to higher the service level for the customer, the systems does so by optimizing efficiency of processes.

Orderadministration is the processing of order flows. The process of administrating orders is needed for other actors within a business to function. Orderadministration follows the process, thus acts on multiple levels. The function starts at the accepting of an order, and finishes when the invoice has been paid.

×

Stock management is a function within a company that provides having stock when needed and maintaining stock levels for future demand. The goal of stockmanagement is to optimize the service level while lowering the stock costs. Stockmanagement can be handled centralized or decentralized variable for the size of the company.

×

Crossdocking is a process where a combined shipment or multiple shipments get send to a warehouse, where the goods are then unloaded and directly loaded onto new trucks. This process causes routes to be split into multiple routes when needed by the help of warehouses. The main process of crossdocking is done physically within warehouses.
